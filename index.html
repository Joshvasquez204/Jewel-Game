Jewel Collector

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four jewels below. By clicking on a jewel you will add a amount of points to your total score.
  • You will win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each jewel is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each jewel.
